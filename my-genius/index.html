Welcome to the Pacific Northwest... first stop, skiing!

introduction

If you've never heard of skiing, you are obviously missing out. Skiing is the most freeing, slightly frightening, exiting thing I have ever experienced. Believe me, I know it can seem intimidating and a little scary, but it truly is a matter of just starting and not letting your fear get in the way.

Maybe you still aren't convinced... and that's fine! But, if you keep reading, I'll teach you the basics anyways :)

basics

In short, when you are skiing, you have boots and you have skis. The easiest way to stop when you are first leaning is to just make a wedge shape with your skis. To go faster, you keep them parallel. Finally, to start turning, shift your weight to your left ski to turn right and vice versa.

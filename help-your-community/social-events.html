Social Events

SOCIAL EVENTS

Social events at EPS let students connect with each other and hang out in supervised, fun enviroments. These events come in a varitety of forms, from events at school to events at outside locations, such as the ice rink in the Winter Social.

Here's a little about some current social events at our school.


Homecoming

Homecoming is a celebration of community, typically it is the season where past members of a community are welcomed back, but a celebration of community nonetheless. This year is extra special because EPS is celebrating our 20th anniversary. Come enjoy an evening of music, fun, and relaxation with friends. This is our first dance of the year and open to all of Upper School (9-12). There will be a DJ, refreshments, and photo booths for use!



Spirit Week

Your chance to show your support and spirit for EPS! Each day of the week come dressed according to the theme provided for an opportunity to earn spirit points! Themes were voted on prior during Eaglecon and from your suggestions.


Coffee House

The Coffee House is an open mic night where all students are welcome to share their work in a supportive space of peers. In the past, we have had students share original and cover songs (both live and recorded), comedy, poetry, as well as having a featured visual artist that works on a piece during the performances and shows off the finished product by the end of the night.
